About Agency

Agency (law), a person acting on behalf of another person. Agency (LDS Church), "the privilege of choice... introduced by God". Agency (moral), capacity for making moral judgments. Agency (philosophy), the capacity of an autonomous agent to act, relating to action theory in philosophy.
Agency (sociology) In social science, agency is the capacity of individuals to act independently and to make their own free choices. By contrast, structure is those factors of influence (such as social class, religion, gender, ethnicity, ability, customs, etc.) that determine or limit an agent and their decisions.
Government agency, a department of a local or national government responsible for the oversight and administration of a specific function. International agency, an inter-governmental body. Marketing agency, a service business dedicated to creating, planning and handling marketing for its clients.
